OUR STORY

Interior Visualizer is an innovative startup.
We had an idea for interior purchasing application but no platform for executing it. Then our story begins with us availing an opportunity provided for innovative ideas for startups at National Expansion Plan of NICS. Our mission is to provide augmented view to our customers of how a certain interior furnishings will look at different places before actually buying it. We pitched our idea and started executing it. We work together to design, innovate, implement and market our idea.
